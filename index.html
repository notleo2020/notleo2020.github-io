Steal a Seal - Features

🥽

Full VR Immersion

Experience seal-stealing like never before with hand tracking, room-scale movement, and full VR immersion designed exclusively for virtual reality.

🌊

Immersive Underwater VR

Dive into beautifully crafted VR underwater environments where you can physically reach out and interact with seals in 3D space.

👐

Hand Tracking Controls

Use natural hand movements to catch and steal seals with intuitive VR controls that make you feel like you're really there.

🎯

Room-Scale Gameplay

Move around your play space to sneak up on seals, dodge obstacles, and explore underwater caves in full room-scale VR.

🔊

3D Spatial Audio

Hear seals splashing around you with immersive 3D audio that helps you locate and track your targets in the virtual ocean.

🏆

VR Achievements

Unlock special VR achievements by performing physical gestures and completing challenges that only work in virtual reality.
